### 🎯 Key Evaluation Focus Areas

#### 1. Upstream Quality & Value Chain Efficiency
* Assess how raw hide and skin processing quality influences down-stream leather manufacturing and high-tier competitive advantages.
* Track efficiency improvements across target tanneries following major infrastructure modifications.

#### 2. Institutional Capacity & Policy Shifts
* Map systemic policy shifts across institutional regional partners to verify project sustainability.
* Evaluate employment creation metrics across key industrial development corridors.
