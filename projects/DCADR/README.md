### 🎯 Core Program Objectives & Indicators

#### 1. Resolution Velocity & System Efficiency
* Track the average lifecycle duration of cases routed through Alternative Dispute Resolution (ADR) protocols.
* Measure user satisfaction variance across distinct institutional case types.

#### 2. ASAL Regional Context & Stability Monitoring
* Map systemic performance across fragile or highly volatile ASAL (Arid and Semi-Arid Lands) corridors.
* Build rapid data loop mechanisms to monitor institutional delivery resilience during localized environmental or logistical supply shocks.
